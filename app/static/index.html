ℹ️ About JanSevaEnv

JanSevaEnv is an RL environment for diagnosing Indian welfare and pension grievances. Agents investigate cases by asking diagnostic questions, then submit a root cause and resolution. Rewards are shaped to encourage efficient, accurate diagnosis across three difficulty levels: Easy (PM-KISAN), Medium (OAP/WAP), and Hard (MGNREGA).
